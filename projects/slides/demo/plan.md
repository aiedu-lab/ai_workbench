# Demo Plan: AI Workbench — 5-Slide Pitch Deck

Use this plan with [Gamma](https://gamma.app) to generate the
instructor demo deck live during the Slides session. Paste the
prompt below directly into Gamma's "Generate" dialog.

---

## Gamma Prompt

```
Create a 5-slide presentation titled "AI Workbench".
Audience: high school and university students attending the lab.
Tone: energetic, practical, jargon-free.
Constraint: exactly 5 slides, one central idea per slide,
no more than 3 bullet points per slide.

Slide 1 — Who We Are
Title: "Who Runs This Lab — and Who It's For"
Content:
- Instructor-led, community-driven program
- Open to high school and university students
- No prior AI experience required

Slide 2 — Why This Lab Exists
Title: "Why: From Curious to Capable"
Content:
- AI tools are reshaping how software is built
- Most courses teach theory — this lab teaches practice
- Goal: leave able to build real AI-powered workflows

Slide 3 — What We Build Together
Title: "What: One Project, Six Versions"
Content:
- Group Meetup Organizer — grows session by session
- Starts as a pitch deck; ends as a deployed Docker stack
- Same project: six levels of sophistication

Slide 4 — How to Contribute
Title: "How: Every Session Leaves a Trace"
Content:
- Add your best prompts to /prompts/best.md
- Log what failed and why in /prompts/failures.md
- Commit session notes to /learnings/session_notes/
- Volunteer to add more sessions, content, and/or teach new tools.
- Contribute by donating tokens for kids to learn AI tools

Slide 5 — Call to Action
Title: "Your Turn — Let's Build"
Content:
- Open your laptop and follow along
- Ask questions out loud — others have the same ones
- The only wrong move is not trying
```

---

## Validation Checklist

After generating the deck in Gamma, verify before presenting:

- [ ] Exactly 5 slides (no more, no fewer)
- [ ] Each slide has exactly one central idea
- [ ] No slide exceeds 3 bullet points
- [ ] Slide 3 names the Group Meetup Organizer explicitly
- [ ] Slide 4 references actual repo paths (`/prompts/`, `/learnings/`)
- [ ] Visual theme is consistent across all slides
- [ ] You can explain every slide without reading it aloud

---

## Instructor Notes

- Build this deck **live** in Gamma during the session — students
  watch the AI generate it in real time; that IS the demo.
- After generating: show one **Edit** iteration (change a bullet,
  adjust the theme) to demonstrate Gamma's edit-vs-regenerate flow.
- Time budget: 10 minutes to generate + 5 minutes to iterate.
