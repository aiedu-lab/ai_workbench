## Curated high quality prompts from sessions

### Prompting

#### CreditCard 

##### Prompt
```bash
Context:
Audience is a 12-year-old with no financial domain knowledge.

Task:
Explain how a credit card works.

Constraints:
- Use simple language
- Use an analogy
- Avoid financial jargon
- Keep it under 150 words

Output:
- 1 short paragraph
- 1 simple example
```

##### NegativePrompt
```bash
Do NOT: 
- use financial jargon eg APR, interest rate, credit score
- use analogies
- use complex examples
- exceed 150 words
```
