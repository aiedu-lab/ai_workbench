# REPLAN PROMPT

Use the below prompt to effect any changes to 
[Specification Plan](sdw/plan.md)

## PREPARE

Read 
* [Specification Plan](sdw/plan.md) and 
* [Replan Prompt](sdw/prompt_history.md#workbench-contribution-guidelines).

## TEST
What is the title of the section in prompt_history and what line numbers
do they span in that file? What would the file and what would be the
Phase number you'll use to update the `specification plan`?

## OBJECTIVE
Once we confirm you understand the section with the replan prompt:

1. Generate the appends to the specification in the form of New Phases 
and New Steps within those Phases to `Specification Plan` in order 
to meet the requirements. 

2. Once the `Specification Plan` appends are approved, effect the 
update to `Specification Plan` and check off the relevant 
`Replan Prompt` section marking it processed.

## CONSTRAINTS
Under any circumstances:
* Goal is ONLY to update `Specification Plan` to satisfy `Replan Prompt`
* Do NOT execute any steps in the `Specification Plan`
* Do NOT effect any changes to `Specification Plan` unless explicitly approved.
